About Us

At GymTroll, we’re more than just a fitness app — we’re your training partner, your progress tracker, and your personal motivator. Designed by a team of passionate athletes, certified trainers, and tech experts, our goal is to make high-quality fitness guidance accessible to everyone, no matter where they are on their journey.

We believe that fitness should be simple, effective, and sustainable. Whether you're lifting for strength, training to lose fat, or just trying to stay active, our app gives you the tools to build a plan that works for you. From customized workout programs and macro tracking to progress photos and performance analytics, everything is designed to help you stay focused and consistent.

What sets us apart?

Personalized Programs: Tailored workouts based on your goals, experience, and available equipment.

Smart Nutrition Tools: Track calories, macros, and meals with ease.

Progress Monitoring: Visualize your transformation with graphs, photos, and performance tracking.

Community Support: Connect with like-minded users, share tips, and celebrate achievements.

Our mission is to empower you to train with purpose — whether that means smashing PRs, shedding extra pounds, or building lifelong habits. We’re here to support every step, set, and sweat along the way.

Welcome to GymTroll — your fitness, your way.
